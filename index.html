Track your remaining usage ata glance.

UseReserve is a lightweight macOS menu bar app that keeps your remaining Codex and Claude usage visible without interrupting your work.

Buy me coffee

Open Codex and Claude Desktop once, then launch UseReserve to see the limits available on your Mac. Claude may request one-time permission to read your local sign-in.

UseReserve is read-only. It stores only presentation data—percentages, reset dates, and refresh state—and never stores credentials, cookies, or message content.

Menu bar
Open UseReserve again if its icon is missing. If your menu bar is crowded, close an unused menu-bar app so macOS has room to show it.
Sign-in
Open Codex or Claude Desktop and confirm you are signed in, then return to UseReserve.
Claude permission
Choose Connect Claude in UseReserve and approve access to Claude Safe Storage when macOS asks.
Stale readings
UseReserve keeps the last available reading during a temporary outage and shows when it was last updated.
Refresh
Open the more menu and choose Refresh after reconnecting to the internet or signing in again.
Login Items
If UseReserve does not start automatically, open System Settings → General → Login Items and enable UseReserve.
